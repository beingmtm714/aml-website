For Artisans

If your work makes
your city what it is,
you're an artisan.

We use "artisan" the way the guilds did: not a hobby, a craft that pays.

Who qualifies

A creative business that adds to the character of a place.

Makers
  • Furniture maker
  • Ceramicist at the market
  • Glassblower, leatherworker
Food & drink
  • Café, brewery, restaurant
  • Food pop-up chasing its first storefront
Media & print
  • Record label
  • Print shop

What they share: real overhead, real customers, a product with a point of view. The test is culture, not medium. Does this business help make the city what it is?
